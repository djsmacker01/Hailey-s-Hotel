Hotel Amenities

Enhancing your stay with premium facilities and services

Experience Luxury & Comfort

Recreation & Relaxation

Swimming Pool

Our temperature-controlled swimming pool offers year-round refreshment. Surrounded by comfortable loungers and a pool bar service, it's the perfect place to unwind.

Hours: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Fitness Center

Stay on track with your fitness goals at our fully-equipped fitness center, featuring cardio equipment, free weights, and dedicated space for yoga and stretching.

Hours: 24/7 Access with Room Key

Spa & Wellness

Treat yourself to ultimate relaxation with our spa services, including massages, facials, and body treatments. Our skilled therapists ensure a rejuvenating experience.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Appointments recommended)

Dining

The Coastal Restaurant

Enjoy fine dining with local and international cuisine prepared by our expert culinary team. Featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients and an extensive wine list.

Breakfast: 6:30 AM - 10:30 AM
Dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Lobby Café & Bar

Perfect for casual dining, our café offers light meals, snacks, and a variety of beverages throughout the day. Evening cocktails and local spirits are available at the bar.

Café: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Bar: 12:00 PM - 12:00 AM

Room Service

Enjoy our restaurant menu from the comfort of your room with our prompt and professional room service. Perfect for a private dining experience or late-night cravings.

Available 24/7
Full menu: 6:30 AM - 10:00 PM
Limited menu: 10:00 PM - 6:30 AM

Business & Events

Business Center

Our well-equipped business center provides computers, printing services, and high-speed internet to keep you connected during your stay.

Hours: 24/7 Access with Room Key

Conference Rooms

Host successful meetings in our versatile conference spaces, equipped with modern audio-visual equipment and supported by our dedicated events team.

Available by reservation

Banquet Hall

Perfect for weddings, celebrations, and corporate events, our elegant banquet hall can accommodate up to 200 guests with customizable catering options.

Available by reservation

Convenience Services

Concierge Services

Let our knowledgeable concierge team assist with transportation arrangements, tour bookings, restaurant reservations, and local recommendations.

Available 24/7 at the Front Desk

Laundry & Dry Cleaning

Keep your wardrobe fresh with our laundry and dry cleaning services. Same-day service available for items received before 9:00 AM.

Available daily

Transportation

Convenient airport transfers and local transportation can be arranged through our front desk, with options including our hotel shuttle service and car rentals.

Shuttle service runs hourly from 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
